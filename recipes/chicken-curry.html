Chicken curry

Chicken curry: 4 servings

Chicken pieces are cooked in an aromatic, deliciously curry sauce then it's finished off with cream for richness and cilantro for color and freshness

Serve alongside basmati rice or naan for soaking up the extra sauce

Ingredients

Spice blend

  • 1 1/2 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 stp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp fennel seeds, crushed in a small bag with a meal mallet
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp ground mustard
  • 1/4 tsp ground cloves

Curry

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small yellow onion, chopped (1 cup)
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced (1 1/2 tbsp)
  • 1 tbsp peeled and minced fresh ginger
  • 1 cup low-sodium chicken broth
  • 3/4 cup drained canned diced tomatoes or peeled, seeded, diced fresh tomatos
  • Salt
  • Cayenne pepper, to taste
  • 680g boneless skinless chicken breasts, diced into cubes
  • 1 tsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tsp water (optional)
  • 1/3 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp chopped cilantro
